Cover for G9200-XXXX inverters which suit L210 transmission
Suits lugs up to 35mm^2 but will require grinding off part of the lug on the right hand side (+) port due to limited clearance.
This connector is:
- Side exit, cables routed towards the coolant inlets.
- Low profile (approx 30mm height) to suit my use case.
- Deisgned to be waterproof though effect may be mediocre,
  - install 3mm section O-ring cord along the seam,
  - ~2mm section oring on the cables, may vary to suit differing cable diam
  - ~3x20ish mm oring on the bottom to seal between the inverter and
